Our Wood Polishing Process

Assessment

We begin with a thorough evaluation of your wooden surfaces, considering factors such as type of wood, existing finish, and desired outcome.

Preparation

The surfaces are carefully cleaned, sanded, and repaired to ensure a smooth and even foundation for the polish.

Polishing

Based on the assessment, we apply the appropriate polishing method, which may include staining, varnishing, or waxing.

Finishing

Once the polish is applied, we add the finishing touches to bring out the natural beauty of the wood.

Quality Check

Our contractors conduct a comprehensive quality check to ensure that the final result meets our high standards and your expectations.
