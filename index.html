Questions

Frequently asked questions?

What types of painting services do you offer?

We offer both residential and commercial painting services including interior, exterior, wood polishing, waterproofing, and more.

Do you help with color selection?

Yes! Our experts provide color consultation to help you choose the perfect shades based on lighting, style, and preferences.

Are your paints safe and eco-friendly?

Absolutely. We offer low-VOC and eco-friendly paint options that are safe for children, pets, and the environment.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on project size, but most residential jobs are completed within 2–5 days. We always provide a clear schedule before starting.

Do I need to vacate my home or office during the painting?

Not necessarily. We plan work in stages to minimize disruption. For certain jobs (like polishing or high-fume areas), temporary relocation may be suggested.

What prep work do you handle before painting?

We cover all basic prep—cleaning, sanding, priming, and protecting furniture and floors. We also repair minor cracks and surface flaws.

Is there a warranty on your services?

Yes. We offer a workmanship warranty (typically 1 year) depending on the project. Full details are shared in your quote.

How can I get a quote?

You can request a free estimate through our website, WhatsApp, or by calling us directly. Site visits can also be scheduled if needed.
